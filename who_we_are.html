WHO ARE WE

Escapist is an Aarhus-based event company, established in the spring of 2017.
At Escapist, we focus on creating unique and exciting gae experiences for people who are ready to have their wits, teamwork and communication skills challenged.
Find our first five-star project, the two escape rooms USS Triton and The Great Diamon Heist, on Klostergade in Aarhus, and look forward to more entertaining conceps in the future.
