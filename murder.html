Murder at Snowpeak

Sir Jeffrey has been murdered, and only one thing is certain - one of you did it! The murder of Snowpeak is a murder mystery that needs to be solved at home in the living room. Each player will take on the role of one of the 6 unique characters with different quirks and secrets. One of the players is secretly the killer, who must hold the mask through the game's 4 acts while traces and mysterious objects are found. Can you expose the killer and let justice be done to the fullest? Or will the culprit go free? Get ready for an evening you will never forget…
  • Recommended age: 13+
  • Playing time: 3-5 hours
  • Price: 249, -
  • NOTE: Must be played by exactly 6 players. Can only be played once by the same group.
